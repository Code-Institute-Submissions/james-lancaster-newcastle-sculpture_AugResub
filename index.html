Cities and towns the world over are adorned with public sculpture. Much of the time it gets sadly little attention from passers-by. It’s worth stopping to take a look, though, because not only are there some wonderful artistic gems to enjoy, there’s lots to be learned about local history, and about an area’s people and communities.

Newcastle upon Tyne is home to a collection of wonderful public sculpture. In particular, it has some excellent modern work that articulates its industrial, cultural, and maritime heritage.

This website will guide you to the best of this, and a few other odds and ends along the way.

Have a look at the locations of interesting sculptures and plan an itinerary to visit them. Test your knowledge of Newcastle's sculptures. And do join the sculpture loving community and stay in touch with us.
